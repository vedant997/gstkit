How it works

1. Enter the GST-inclusive amount.

2. Select the applicable GST rate.

3. Click Calculate Reverse GST to get the base price and tax split instantly.

Formulas

Base Amount: Inclusive Amount × 100 ÷ (100 + GST Rate)

GST Amount: Inclusive Amount - Base Amount

CGST: GST ÷ 2, SGST: GST ÷ 2, IGST: GST Amount

FAQ

How do I calculate reverse GST?

Enter the GST-inclusive amount, choose the GST rate, and calculate to see the original base amount instantly.

What is the reverse GST formula?

Base Amount = Inclusive Amount × 100 ÷ (100 + GST Rate). GST Amount = Inclusive Amount - Base Amount.

Does it show CGST and SGST?

Yes, CGST, SGST, and IGST values are calculated and displayed automatically.

Is this reverse GST calculator free?

Yes, it is free to use on desktop and mobile.
